About Domestic Cats

They are often called house cats when kept as indoors or simply cats when there is no need to distinguish them from other fileds and felines. They are often valued by humans for companionship.

There are more than seventy cats breeds recognized by various cat registries..

Cat senses
  • They can hear sounds too faint or too high in frequency for human ears
  • They can see in near darkness
  • They have poorer color vision and a better sense of smell than humans.
